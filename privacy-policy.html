Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Mehrab Hall ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us through this website.

Information We Collect

When you submit a booking enquiry, we collect your name, phone number, email address, event details, and any message you provide. We do not collect payment information through this website.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to booking enquiries and provide event quotations.
  • To coordinate event planning details with you and your event team.
  • To send confirmations and updates related to your booking.

WhatsApp & Third-Party Communication

Our booking form opens a pre-filled WhatsApp conversation to speed up your enquiry. Messages sent this way are subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policy in addition to ours.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Details are shared only with internal staff involved in planning your event.

Cookies

This site may use minimal functional cookies or local storage to remember your preferences (such as menu tab selections). No third-party advertising trackers are used.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at events@mehrabhall.com.
