Our Story

A Legacy Built on Every Vow Spoken Here

Since 2009, Mehrab Hall has been Lahore's sanctuary for grand celebrations — where Mughal-inspired archways, hand-finished gold detailing, and warm Punjabi hospitality come together to create an atmosphere no other venue can replicate.

Our mission is simple: to relieve families of the stress of hosting and hand them, instead, an evening they will describe for decades. Every marble floor, every chandelier, every dish leaving our kitchen is chosen with that promise in mind.

Royal Interiors

Hand-carved detailing and imported drapery throughout.

Five-Star Catering

Dedicated Planners

A personal coordinator from booking to the last dance.

Complete Peace of Mind

Security, valet, and backup power, always included.
